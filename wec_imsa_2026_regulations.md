# 2026 WEC and IMSA Driver Regulations

## Universal Regulations
Both series enforce a strict maximum drive time limit: **A driver may not exceed 4 hours of seat time within any rolling 6-hour window.**

---

## 2026 IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship
Red flag periods during the second half of a race, or cumulative red flags exceeding 25% of the race distance, trigger a 50% reduction in all minimum drive-time requirements.

| Event Duration | Drivers Allowed | GTP / GTD PRO Min | LMP2 / GTD Min | Total Max Drive Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **24 Hours** (Daytona) | 3-5 (GTP max 4) | 2h 00m | 4h 30m | 13h 00m |
| **12 Hours** (Sebring) | 3-4 | 1h 00m | 3h 00m | 8h 00m |
| **10 Hours** (Petit Le Mans) | 3-4 | 45m | 2h 30m | 7h 00m |
| **6 Hours** | 2-3 | 30m | 1h 30m | 4h 30m |
| **2h 40m** | 2 | 10m | 45m | Limited by co-driver min |
| **100m** | 2 | 5m | 35m | Limited by co-driver min |

*Note: 2026 Daytona Exception: GTD PRO regulations require any Bronze-rated driver approved for the Pro class to fulfill a 3.5-hour minimum drive time.*

## 2026 FIA World Endurance Championship (WEC)
To score WEC championship points, drivers must meet minimum seat-time thresholds. In LMGT3, the minimum drive times specifically target the Bronze and Silver-rated drivers in the lineup.

| Event Duration | Drivers Allowed | Hypercar Min | LMGT3 Min (Bronze/Silver) | Total Max Drive Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **24 Hours** (Le Mans) | 3 | 1h 00m | 6h 00m | 14h 00m |
| **8 Hours / 1812km** | 3 | 1h 00m | 2h 15m | 5h 15m |
| **6 Hours** | 2-3 (3 req for LMGT3) | 1h 00m | 1h 45m | 4h 00m |
